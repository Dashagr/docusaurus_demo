Logo and description of your marketplace on the payment page

For the payer, the standard payment form will look like this (the screenshot shows a version of the form where only payment by bank cards is connected):

A standard payment page

But you can make it more personalized by adding the logo and a few words about your marketplace in the checkout settings. For this make a few steps done.

Step 1. Go to the checkout settings​

In the 'My Checkouts' section, select the desired checkout and click on the settings icon.

Settings icon

Step 2. Attach a logotype and add a description​

In the 'General settings' section, attach a logo, an image in png or jpeg format. Standard size is 200x40 px. And fill in the 'Description' field – this text will be displayed on the payment page under the logo.

Marketplace description and logo

Congratulations! After these actions, the header of your payment page will contain more information about you and will look like this:

Added merchant logo and description on the payment page
